# OUTREACH_RULES.md

## Contact-Safety Gate (must pass before every send)
- [ ] Public business email found
- [ ] Email source URL recorded
- [ ] No "do not contact" warning
- [ ] Message relevant to business
- [ ] Business not in avoided industry
- [ ] Sender identity in email
- [ ] Opt-out/unsubscribe language included
- [ ] Outreach logged in system
- [ ] Prototype watermarked + demo-locked
- [ ] No false claims
- [ ] No impersonation of official business website

## First Email Structure
1. Personalized greeting (use business name)
2. "I noticed your business could benefit from a modern website"
3. "I made an unofficial preview concept for you"
4. Screenshot/thumbnail (small, hosted)
5. Preview link (watermarked, demo-locked)
6. "This is an unofficial preview concept, not your live website"
7. Light CTA: "Would you like it finalized?"
8. No pricing
9. Opt-out: "Reply STOP to opt out"

## A/B Email Angles
Rotate these and track conversion:
- **Direct:** "I made you a website preview"
- **Benefit:** "Your business deserves a better online presence"
- **Social proof:** "I've helped similar Canadian businesses get online"
- **Curiosity:** "I built something for [Business Name] — want to see it?"

## Follow-Up Rules
- One email follow-up after 5 days if no reply
- Stop after no response to follow-up
- Stop immediately on negative reply
- Stop immediately on opt-out
- SMS follow-up only for 80+ leads, only after email attempt, only once

## SMS Rules (Telnyx +18253953636)
- Only for leads scoring 80+
- Only after email has been sent and 5+ days passed with no reply
- Brief: "Hi [Name], I sent you a website preview for [Business]. Want to see it? [Link]"
- Only one SMS per lead
- Stop on no reply or negative response
- Log all SMS sends
