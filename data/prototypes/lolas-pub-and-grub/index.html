About Lola's Pub & Grub

Lola's Pub & Grub is a beloved local restaurant in Cornwall, Ontario, where neighbors gather over great food and cold drinks. With a welcoming atmosphere, generous portions, and a menu that mixes pub favorites with creative specials, we've become a go-to spot for families, friends, and anyone craving a hearty meal in a relaxed setting.
