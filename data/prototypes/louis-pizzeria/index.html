About Louis' Pizzeria

Welcome to Louis' Pizzeria, a beloved local restaurant in Cornwall, Ontario. For years, we've been serving the community authentic, wood-fired pizzas, fresh pasta, and homemade Italian comfort food. Our passion is simple: quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a warm, welcoming table for every guest who walks through our door. Whether you're a longtime local or just passing through, we'd love to feed you.
