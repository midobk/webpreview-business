The Chair

A shop that takes its time.

Chop Hair Design has been a Pitt Street fixture for over a decade — a small room, two chairs, the hum of a clipper, a radio you can hear. No upsell, no rush, no surprises on the bill.

If you've been coming in for a while, you know the routine. If you're new: pull up, take a seat, we'll figure out the cut before the cape goes on.

“Good hair is patient hair.”

What to Expect

Three things we always do.

01 · THE CONSULT

We talk before we cut.

Length, shape, what you liked last time, what you didn't. Five minutes at the mirror saves twenty in the chair.

02 · THE WASH

Hot towel, warm water.

Every cut includes a wash and a hot towel. Beard or shave services get the full lather treatment — pre-shave oil, brush, hot cloth.

03 · THE FINISH

Neck and edges, clean.

Straight-razor neck clean-up, lined cheek and sideburn, a quick style, a check in the mirror. You leave looking finished, not just trimmed.
