Our Story

Birchwood Restaurant & Bar is a beloved local restaurant in Cornwall, Ontario, where community and cuisine come together. We're passionate about showcasing the best of our region's ingredients, supporting nearby farms and producers, and creating a welcoming space where friends, families, and visitors can enjoy exceptional food, handcrafted drinks, and the kind of warm hospitality that makes you feel right at home.
