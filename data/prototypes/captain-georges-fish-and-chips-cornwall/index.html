About Captain George's

Located in the heart of Cornwall, Ontario, Captain George's Fish & Chips has been serving up the freshest, crispiest fish and chips in the region. We're a proud local restaurant dedicated to bringing families and friends together over great food. Every piece of fish is hand-battered using our family recipe, cooked to golden perfection, and served with a smile. Whether you're dining in, taking out, or feeding the whole family, we're your neighborhood spot for a true Cornwall tradition.
