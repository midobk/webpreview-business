The Shop

A chair, a cape, a straight answer.

Barber Shop is the kind of place where the barber remembers what you got last time. Four chairs, a hot towel station, and the same steady hands behind the clippers every visit.

  • Walk-ins welcome — book ahead if you want a guaranteed chair.
  • Clean tools, fresh cape, and a real consultation before the clippers come up.
  • Cash, debit, and card. Tap friendly.
The Craft

Built around your hair, not the other way around.

Every cut starts with a quick look — hair type, growth pattern, the way it falls when you forget about it. Then the chair, the cape, and the clippers. No rushing, no small talk you didn't ask for.

  • Skin fades, tapers, scissor cuts and beard work.
  • Hot towel shaves with a straight razor, by request.
  • Seniors and students — ask about the standing rate.
