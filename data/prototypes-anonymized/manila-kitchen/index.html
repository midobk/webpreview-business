About Us

Local Restaurant & Café is a local restaurant in Cornwall, Ontario, bringing the vibrant tastes of the Philippines to the Seaway Valley. We pride ourselves on fresh ingredients, generous portions, and the warm hospitality our kababayans know and love. Whether you're craving a taste of home or trying Filipino food for the first time, our kitchen has a seat waiting for you.
