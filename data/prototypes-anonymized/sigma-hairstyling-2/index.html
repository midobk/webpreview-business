About the chair

One salon, one room, one good conversation.

Barber Shop has been part of the Danis Avenue block in Cornwall for years. The shop is small on purpose — a handful of chairs, a window that lets the afternoon in, and stylists who like the work.

We don't run promotions, and we don't push add-ons. The goal is a cut you'll still like six weeks from now, with a colour that fades gracefully rather than growing out in stripes.
