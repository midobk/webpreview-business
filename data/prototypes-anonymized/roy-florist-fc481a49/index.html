How an order works

Three steps, no surprises.

From the first message to the handoff — here is what to expect when you order.

01

BATCH — ORDER

Tell us about the occasion

Drop a note with the date, the recipient, the feeling you want, and a budget. We will come back with what is in season and what fits.

02

BATCH — ARRANGE

We build around the day

For weddings and events we sketch the palette, source the stems, and confirm the count a week out. For everyday orders, we work with what walked in that morning.

03

BATCH — HANDOFF

Pick up or delivered

Pick up from the shop, or have it delivered in water. Wedding and event work is delivered and set up on the day.

Seasonal note

What is in right now.

The cooler changes with the week, and so does the work on the counter. Right now the stems we are reaching for are the ones that hold up in the heat and the ones that photograph at golden hour — peonies, garden roses, ranunculus, eucalyptus, dusty miller, soft hydrangea. If you have a colour or a feeling in mind, say so and we will work around what is freshest.
