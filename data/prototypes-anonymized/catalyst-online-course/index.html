The curriculum

Six weeks. One transformation.

Each week builds on the last. By week 6, you'll have a complete operating system for the next decade of your life.

Week 01

Name what's actually happening

We start by getting radically honest. Most people have never articulated what's really keeping them stuck. This week, you will — in writing.

  • The "Honest Inventory" exercise
  • Identifying your real blockers (not the surface ones)
  • 1:1 kickoff call with your coach
Week 02

Design the person, not the goal

Goals don't change behavior. Identity does. We rebuild your self-concept from the ground up — and suddenly, action gets easy.

  • The Identity Stack framework
  • Writing your "Future Self" letter
  • Live workshop: designing your week
Week 03

Reclaim your attention

Your phone has more of your mind than you do. This week, we take it back — and discover what you've been avoiding when you're not scrolling.

  • The Deep Work Stack method
  • Phone setup, browser setup, room setup
  • First 3-hour focus block, coached live
Week 04

Master your energy

Time management is a lie. Energy management is the real game. We rebuild your sleep, your mornings, and your recovery — with science, not hustle.

  • The Energy Audit
  • Building your non-negotiable morning
  • Live Q&A with a sleep specialist
Week 05

Build the body that builds the life

Your body is the substrate. We don't do 6am workouts or impossible diets — we build a sustainable, energizing baseline that lasts decades.

  • The "Minimum Viable Athlete" framework
  • Strength + mobility starter plan
  • Nutrition principles, not rules
Week 06

Make it stick — for life

Six weeks isn't a transformation, it's a launch. We end by designing the next year — and the systems that will keep you moving when the novelty fades.

  • Your 12-month Life Design Plan
  • Final 1:1 coaching call
  • Alumni community access — forever

Frequently asked.

How much time do I need each week?
Plan for about 4–5 hours per week: 90 minutes for the live call, 60 minutes of exercises, and 1–2 hours of reading/practice. The work is light, but it compounds if you actually do it.
What if I miss a live call?
Every call is recorded within 4 hours and posted to your dashboard. You'll also get a written summary of the key insights. The only thing you can't replay is the breakout conversations — those are sacred.
Is this therapy?
No — and Maya will tell you to keep seeing your therapist if you have one. This is a coaching program, focused on practical tools, identity work, and forward motion. We stay in our lane.
What's the refund policy?
Full refund within 30 days, no questions asked. If you do the work and don't feel like you've gotten 10x the value, you get your money back. We've refunded 2% of students — ever.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes. The Live Cohort and VIP paths can be split into 12 monthly payments at no extra cost. The Self-paced tier is one-time only.
When's the next cohort?
Cohort 21 starts July 8, 2026. We run 3–4 cohorts per year. Once a cohort fills (50 spots), we close applications — so apply early if you have a specific start date in mind.
