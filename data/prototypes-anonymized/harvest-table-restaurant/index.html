A family affair

"We cook what we grew up eating. Nothing more, nothing less."

Three generations of the Aubert family worked the kitchens of Brittany's coastal auberges before Camille opened the doors of Maison du Sel in 2014. Her grandmother's copper pots still hang above the pass.

Today, the restaurant holds one Michelin star, a 28-seat dining room, and a stubborn commitment to the seasons — the menu changes every six weeks, and not a day sooner.

C
Camille Aubert
Chef & Owner
