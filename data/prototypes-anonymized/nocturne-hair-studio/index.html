The visit

Unhurried, on purpose.

The consult

Ten minutes before any water runs — photos welcome, honesty guaranteed, and the plan agreed before the gown goes on.

ii.

The chair

One client per stylist, start to finish. Your colour isn't processing while somebody else gets a cut.

iii.

The finish

Styled the way you'll actually wear it, with the products named so you can do it again at home — or not, and come back Friday.

The room

Three chairs, low light, good records on.

Nocturne is a three-chair studio downtown — brick wall, warm lamps, and the kind of playlist you ask about. Evenings are the house specialty: the last appointments start at seven, so a workday doesn't cost you your hair.

Walk-ins get honest answers about the wait. Regulars get their stylist's book a season ahead.

The good mirror is the one by the window.

Finding us

Downtown, up the stairs.

Second floor, downtown Cornwall — the doorway between the café and the bookshop, the one with the brass number. Street parking is easy after six, which is exactly when the late chairs start.
