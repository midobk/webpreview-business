About Mario's

Local Restaurant & Café is a beloved local dining spot in the heart of Cornwall, Ontario. Known for warm hospitality, generous portions, and comforting home-style cooking, we've been bringing families and friends together around the table for years. Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch or celebrating a special occasion, Mario's is where Cornwall comes home.
