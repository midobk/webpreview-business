FIG. 03 — THE PROCESS

Estimate first. Always.

No surprise line items. The price you approve is the price on the invoice.

STEP 01

Drop off

Book a bay, leave the keys, tell us what it's doing. Morning drop-offs are back on the road same day for most jobs.

STEP 02

Approve in writing

We send the finding and the estimate before touching a part. You approve it — or we put the car back together, no charge for looking.

STEP 03

Drive out

Work done to spec, old parts in the trunk if you want them, and the inspection sheet in the glovebox.
